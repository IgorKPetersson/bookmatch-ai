About BookMatch

BookMatch is a student project exploring how artificial intelligence and modern web technologies can be used to create personalized recommendations for books and other types of media.

The goal is to help users discover new content based on what they already enjoy, starting with books and expanding to other media formats in the future.

Future Vision

📚 Books

Intelligent book recommendations based on themes, style, and user preferences.

🎬 Movies

Discover films that match your taste in storytelling, mood, and genre.

🎵 Music

Get music recommendations based on emotional tone and listening habits.

🎭 Theatre & Culture

Personalized recommendations for theatre, shows, and cultural experiences.

Planned Technology Stack

  • React for the frontend
  • FastAPI for the backend
  • AI / LLM-based recommendation logic
  • Databases for storing content and user preferences
